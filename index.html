WELCOME TO MTS NURUL AMAL

MTs Nurul Amal merupakan sekolah Kaligrafi terbagus yang ada di Jakarta
tidak hanya kaligrafi saja kami juga mempersiapkan 5 keunggulan
lainnya untuk mempersiapkan anda menjadi orang sukses

Our Vision

Become a balanced and excellent islamic school in mind, morals, spirit that guided by sunnah of prophet Muhammad SAW

Our Mission

1. organizing balanced education and assessment
2. building harmonious collaboration with all parties involved in guiding students
3. providing useful life skills, especially in Quran reading and writing, memorizing short surahs, and calligraphy
